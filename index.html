Architecture

Three layers of memory.

Inspired by how veteran traders develop expertise — raw experience → pattern recognition → intuitive judgment.

L1 · RAW TRADES

Record Everything

Every trade auto-recorded with full context — entry, exit, SL, TP, session, outcome, and AI reasoning.

record_trade(
  symbol="XAUUSD",
  direction="LONG",
  session="london",
  result=WIN +164p
)
L2 · PATTERNS

Discover What Works

Reflection engine analyzes history, extracts patterns: which strategies win, which sessions to avoid.

london_breakout: 73% win
asia_entries: 52% win
nfp_counter: 23% → avoid
L3 · STRATEGY

Evolve Judgment

Patterns become real-time adjustments. The AI equivalent of "feel for the market."

asia → size × 0.8
london_break → full pos
high_vol → widen stops
